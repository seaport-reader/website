Overview

An established e-commerce company was experiencing declining conversion rates and increased cart abandonment. Users complained about a complicated checkout process and difficulty finding products. The company needed a complete redesign to modernize the platform and improve the overall shopping experience.

My role involved leading the design process from research through implementation, working closely with product managers, developers, and stakeholders to deliver a solution that balanced business goals with user needs.

The Challenge

  • High cart abandonment rate (68%)
  • Outdated, cluttered interface
  • Poor mobile experience
  • Complex checkout flow
  • Low product discovery
Project Hero Image / Before & After Comparison

Research & Discovery

We began with comprehensive user research to understand pain points and opportunities. This included user interviews, usability testing of the existing platform, competitor analysis, and analytics review.

Key Insights

1

Trust Issues

Users felt uncertain about security during checkout, leading to abandonment

2

Information Overload

Too many options and details on product pages confused users

3

Mobile Frustration

Mobile users struggled with small touch targets and slow load times

4

Hidden Costs

Unexpected shipping costs at checkout caused frequent cart abandonment

Design Solution

Based on our research, we developed a comprehensive redesign strategy focused on simplification, trust-building, and mobile optimization.

Streamlined Checkout

Reduced checkout from 6 steps to 3, with guest checkout option and progress indicators. Displayed shipping costs upfront to eliminate surprises.

Enhanced Product Pages

Redesigned product pages with high-quality images, clear CTAs, and simplified information hierarchy. Added user reviews prominently.

Smart Search & Filters

Implemented intelligent search with autocomplete and visual filters that helped users find products 3x faster.

Mobile-First Design

Created a responsive design with thumb-friendly navigation, optimized images, and faster load times.

Trust Signals

Added security badges, clear return policy, and customer testimonials throughout the experience.

Personalization

Integrated recommendation engine that showed relevant products based on browsing history and preferences.

Key Takeaways

  • User research is invaluable - the insights we gained directly informed our most successful design decisions
  • Simplification can be more challenging than adding features, but it's often what users need most
  • Mobile-first thinking improved both mobile and desktop experiences
  • Small trust signals (security badges, reviews, clear policies) have significant impact on conversion
  • Iterative testing and refinement after launch continued to improve metrics
